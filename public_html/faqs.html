Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a Student, Landlord or Agent?

Step 1: Click on Register at the top menu or landlord section.
Step 2: Choose your role (Student, Landlord, or Agent).
Step 3: Fill in your details including name, email, and password.
Step 4: Verify your email address.
Step 5: Once verified, log in and access your dashboard.
– Students can search & apply for hostels.
– Landlords/Agents can list hostels or apartments.

How do I log in and reset my password?

Go to the login page and enter your email and password. If you forget your password, click on Forgot Password and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I search and view available houses?

Use the search bar on the homepage. Enter your preferred location, click Search, and browse the available listings with details, photos, and pricing.

How do I contact a landlord or agent about a listing?

Open a listing and click on the Contact Landlord/Agent button. You can message them directly through the platform.

How do I list a hostel/apartment (Landlord / Agent)?

Log in as a landlord or agent. From your dashboard, click on List Hostel, fill in property details, upload photos, and publish your listing.

How do I look for or post a roommate request?

Go to the Roommate Section from your dashboard. You can either browse roommate requests or create your own request post with your preferences.

Are there fees? How do payments work and how do I stay secure?

Registration is free. Premium features (like highlighted listings) may involve fees. All payments are handled securely through HostelConnect’s payment system. Always communicate and pay within the platform for safety.

How do I report a problem or contact support?

Go to the Help & Support section in your dashboard and submit your issue. Our support team will respond via email.

Troubleshooting: I didn't receive verification email / I can't upload photos / login problems

Please check your spam folder for the verification email. If problems persist, clear your browser cache or try a different browser. For uploads, ensure your files meet the size/format requirements. Contact support if the issue continues.
